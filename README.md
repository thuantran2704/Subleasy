# bookingApp

Initially wanted this to be just another Airbnb clone but decided to take it to a directino that both gives me the chance to learn but also be able to contribute the community instead!

A little bit about the project: as an out of state student, I understand the pain of every student whose parents' house(s) is/are not driving distance to university and they have to sublease their place every summer to not waste some of their hard-earned income. I myself have been struggling with this exact problem. Sure, there are places where people have been doing it such as Marketplace, etc. but searches showing kitchenware and household appliances where you search "apartment for lease" isn't exactly convenient and easy.

So... I've decided to solve this problem by developing a website to address this.
